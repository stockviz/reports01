Key insights are drawn primarily from FY25 Q4 (Justdial) and FY26 Q2 (Matrimony) results, supplemented by investor interactions.

Tailwinds

  • Robust User Traffic & Engagement: Justdial reported 11.8% YoY quarterly unique visitors (191M) and 15% mobile traffic growth (87% of total); B2B traffic share rose to 20%. Matrimony’s ATV grew 10.8% YoY, signaling premiumization.

  • Margin Resilience: Justdial’s EBITDA margin at 29.4% FY25 (vs. 25% target); Matrimony at 10.8% consolidated, with non-marketing margins >57%.

Headwinds

  • Muted Revenue Growth: Justdial FY25 revenue up 9.5% (missed mid-teens target); Q4 at 7% YoY. Matrimony Q2 billing +6.7% YoY, revenue flat (-0.8%), due to billing-revenue gap from long-term packages (to normalize Q4 FY26).

  • Capital Allocation: High cash but delays in dividends/buybacks (Justdial eyes Q1 FY26 policy); diversification (e.g., astrology, jobs) risks cash burn (few crores/quarter).
